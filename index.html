Caestus (2014)

  • Engine: Nebula 3
  • Team size: 10
  • Duration: aprox. 10 weeks
  • Team composition: programmers, artists
  • Role: AI design & programmer, limited network programmer and engine triage

The big game project we did at LTU. It's a 1v1-4 game somewhat similar to Dungeonland for those familiar. Your goal is either to eliminate the heroes by summoning AI minnions or as the heroes to fight your way through the gauntlet to the final boss encounter.

For most of the project I worked with designing and implementing the two different AIs we used; one ranged, stationary support type and one melee swarmer type. Aside from that I also implemented a file reader so we could make scriptable values and also help the artists to place objects into the scene in real-time.
